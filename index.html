Breakout

Breakout is a captivating arcade game that challenges players to break a wall of bricks by deflecting a bouncing ball using a paddle. The player must strategically control the paddle to keep the ball in play and destroy all the bricks. Power-ups occasionally appear, offering benefits such as extended paddle size or multiple balls. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Breakout tests the player’s reflexes and strategic thinking, promising hours of fun and excitement.
